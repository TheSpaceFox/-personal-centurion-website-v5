# Point of difference — talk plainly

You do not need to be a prompt expert to get the most out of Sovereign.

## Public AI chats
Needing a fancy prompt usually means you are fighting the AI — working around training shaped by big tech to seed ideas into the community and train *you*, rather than letting you shape the AI around your life. Prompt theatre is the tax of public AI.

## Sovereign
Ask whatever you want, in ordinary words. It answers as it should. No tip sheets. No prompt engineering hobby. No “expert prompter” requirement.

## Sales language
Prefer: “Just talk to it plainly — like someone who works for you.”
Avoid celebrating prompt tricks as a feature of Sovereign.
