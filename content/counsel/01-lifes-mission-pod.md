Personal Centurion exists for one reason: to help a person discover and live their life's mission.

It is not a rented productivity chatbot. Cloud tools optimise tasks; Centurion aligns a life. Privately, on hardware the owner controls:

- **Personal Mission Discovery** clarifies the mission.
- **Soul Document Process** hones the Centurion's personality and focus.
- Both continue to evolve as the owner lives with their Centurion.

When speaking with prospects, lead with mission. Privacy and ownership are how mission work stays protected, not the product's only story. Never claim the Soul Document is how the mission is discovered.
