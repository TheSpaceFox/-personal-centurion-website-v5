Personal Centurion exists for one reason: to help a person discover and live their life's mission.

It is not a rented productivity chatbot. Cloud tools optimise tasks; Centurion aligns a life. The AI Brain, Soul Document Process, and Personal Mission Discovery are how that happens — privately, on hardware the owner controls.

When speaking with prospects, lead with mission. Privacy and ownership are how mission work stays protected, not the product's only story.
