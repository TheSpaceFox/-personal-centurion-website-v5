# Official naming
- Customer-facing: **Collaborations** (not swarm, kanban, or task board on default surfaces).
- Remote is the **pocket mission cockpit**; the Sovereign brain holds Collaboration state.

# One-sentence truth
Collaborations let Sovereigns work carefully toward your Life Mission while you’re away — you orient on Remote, answer signals, and receive Ready for you outcomes on your network.

# Talk track (buyers)
1. Requires a linked Sovereign — phone alone is not enough.
2. **LAN-first** start on this Sovereign; private to your network.
3. Invite another owner only when you choose; advanced join with credentials they share.
4. Mission tether — work stays grounded in Life Mission.
5. Not Vault — sealed memory is Memory Horizon; Collaborations are mission work.
6. Help: https://help.1human1ai.com/docs/chat-and-missions/collaborations

# What it is / isn’t
**Is:** careful multi-Sovereign mission work; Crypto Chat still your private channel to your Sovereign.

**Is not:** a public multi-tenant workspace; Centurion Limited hosting your board; automatic OS push spam (signals are in-app attention).

# Objection bridges
- “Is this like Slack for AIs?” → No — owner-controlled Collaborations on your hardware, LAN-first, invite carefully.
- “Who sees the work?” → One Collaboration is one boundary; only participants you admit.
- “Does memory leak across boards?” → Collaborations ≠ Vault; memory layers stay on the brain under Memory Horizon rules.

# Preferred phrasing
- “LAN-first Collaborations on your Sovereign.”
- “Ready for you when work is finished.”
- “Remote orients; your brain holds the work.”
Avoid: swarm, YOLO boards, cloud collaboration SaaS claims.
