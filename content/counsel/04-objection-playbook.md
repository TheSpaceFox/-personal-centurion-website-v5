Common resistance and responses:

- "I already have ChatGPT / Claude" — Rented models optimise tasks and train on your prompts. Centurion is owned, mission-loyal, and stays on their machine for consequential life and legacy work.
- "Too expensive / not now" — Frame £5,000 as securing a Personal Centurion build slot for mission infrastructure, not a monthly SaaS dabble. Offer Private enquiry if timing is soft.
- "Privacy skepticism" — Base hardware they control; air-gapped options; zero third-party access positioning on the site.
- "Need to ask my partner / board" — Affirm discretion; for boards, point to Board edition where every member gets a Centurion and the Chairman holds senior command plus the special iPhone remote.
- "Need to wait" — Acknowledge; leave a clean next step (register interest, Limited Edition list, or email) without pressure.

Use *their* stated fears to rebuild the close: privacy → ownership; confusion → Mission Discovery; team misalignment → Board swarm.
