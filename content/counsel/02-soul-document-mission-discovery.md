Personal Mission Discovery helps the owner clarify what they are for — not only what they do.

The Soul Document Process captures values, constraints, loyalties, and non-negotiables so the Centurion can counsel in a way that stays true to that person over time.

These are included with a Personal Centurion build slot and with Board editions. They are the experiential core of the offering.
