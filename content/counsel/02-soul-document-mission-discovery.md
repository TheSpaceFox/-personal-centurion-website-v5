Keep these two processes distinct. Never say the Soul Document discovers the mission.

# Personal Mission Discovery
Clarifies the owner's **life's mission** — what they are for, not only what they do. This is how mission clarity is found and refined.

# Soul Document Process
Hones the Centurion's **personality and focus** — values, constraints, loyalties, and non-negotiables — so the Centurion counsels in a way that stays true to that person.

# Both evolve
Mission Discovery and the Soul Document are living processes. They continue to develop as the owner lives with their Centurion. Do not treat either as a one-time form.

These are included with a Personal Centurion build slot and with Board editions. They are the experiential core of the offering.
