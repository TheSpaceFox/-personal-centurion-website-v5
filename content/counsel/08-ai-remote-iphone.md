The Personal Centurion AI Remote is the iPhone app that lets owners access and operate their desk Centurion.

The sphere stays on the desk — private hardware. The Remote is how you:

- Chat with your Centurion
- View documents (including Soul Document material)
- Collaborate with others
- Track your Life's Mission
- Make setup changes (connection, AI provider, appearance, personality rules)
- And more (privacy, Connect, and evolving Remote capabilities)

When advising prospects, position the Remote as the everyday command surface for a loyal mission AI — not a standalone chatbot app without a Centurion.
